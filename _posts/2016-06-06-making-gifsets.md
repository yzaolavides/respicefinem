During late January, I discovered this musical called "Hamilton". It's about treasury secretary Alexander Hamilton's life. It was written and composed by the genius, Lin Manuel Miranda, who by all accounts has opened my eyes to a new genre of music; rap. The way he incorporated it into a musical was so amazing, and since then I've started a liking to musicals.

So when the last test paper was handed to the proctor during the 18th of March, I immediately went home and started making a gifset of Marvel + Hamilton. I used Hamilton's line:

>If it takes fighting a war for us to meet it will have been worth it.

and related it to the pairing Steve Rogers/Peggy Carter in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. It didn't take more than fifty minutes for me to make since I only had to find scenes in one movie (The First Avenger).

I was satisfied with it, so I decided to stop gif-ing stuff for a while. I spent the rest of the week tying up loose strings on my performance tasks, such as dancing Binislakan and passing my AP scrapbook. Then another idea popped into my mind. 

My love for Star Wars was recently been ignited once more during the release of Force Awakens, and since the movie has been released in Bluray, I figured why the hell not. In this gifset I've used the line "Who lives, who dies who tells your story" and integrated it into the original characters of star wars. Again, it only took about forty-five minutes for me to make this since it all happens in one movie, and I knew what scenes I wanted to gif.
