>It's been two years since I've started my conquest in making a reasonable blog.

The first attempt went quite well, and is currently a fandom blog in Tumblr, sometimes getting recognition, sometimes not. The second ended in a tangle of theme coding mess here in Blogger, and the third ended up being... adequate. Wordpress adequate. But due to my inconsistent motivation, my past self has abandoned it, much to my chagrin.

Now this is my fourth attempt at making a blog, and hopefully this will be my last. It's getting tiring going through numerous theme websites, scrolling and hoping the for the love of everything I'd find something that would suit my taste. besides, fourth time's the charm right?

So welcome, readers, to my blog. I'll post whatever, whenever, wherever. But mostly it's a compilation of my accomplishments during the summer of 2016. Join me as I follow how I got rid of my procrastination and actually do something worthwhile of my time.


That would be all, for now.
